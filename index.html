What is PogChamp.live?

PogChamp.live started out as a personal project with the goal to be able to watch all eSports Twitch.tv streams at the same time on a single screen. Today, the goal remains the same, but I would like to share my creation with the world.

We envision this being used by friends looking to watch all the live tournaments simultaneously on their big screen, or eSports bars to broadcast multiple games on a single screen. Anyone and everyone is welcome to use PogChamp.live, and we greatly value your feedback and suggestions, see our contact info below.

Our mission statement is to be the ultimate eSports viewing experience.
